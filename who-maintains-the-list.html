--- title: Who is providing the custodianship and why? layout: page ---

We need to understand which individual, group or institution is responsible for making sure the a list remains accurate [LINK] and complete [LINK].

Institutional Authority [LINK]

The custodianship of this register is provided by the Register Design Authority, a team in the Government Digital Service part of the Cabinet Office. The Register Design Authority decides which registers are entered onto the register register according to the principles and process set out in these documents.

Legislative Custodianship [LINK]

The custodianship of this list is provided by HM Land Registry who are empowered to perform such functions by legislation including the Land Registration Act 2000 and the Infrastructure Act 2015.

Trusted Observation [LINK]

The custodianship of this list is provided by Democracy Club who have collated and peer-reviewed all 650 Statement of Persons Nominated notices published by local authorities as mandated by the Representation of the People Act 1983 act.
